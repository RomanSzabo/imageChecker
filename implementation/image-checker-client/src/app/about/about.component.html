About

Image Checker is a course project aiming at providing suitable, user-friendly and effective way of checking for duplicate images. The crucial part is storage of needed data. Since individual images are rather large in size, storing of so called key points of the image provides a mean to save only small portion of data which describes needed pieces to compare 2 images.
